Acne Face Map

C H I N
In this spot the breakouts mostly caused by hormonal changes and extreme stressed. Premenstrual breakout are also very common in chin.

C H E E K
This zone often give an accurate glimpse into any respiratory distress. Smokers or people suffering from allergies generally experience fine, broken capillaries or congestion in this area


A R O U N D - M O U T H
If you've had extensive dental surgeries done, you might notice a breakout along the jawline near the ear.


F O R E H E A D - A R E A
This zone represents the bladder and disgestive system. If you breakout frequently in this area, it might means that you need to improve your digestive system by drinking more water and eating more whole, real food and also more fiber


N O S E
A reddened nose can indicate hightened blood pressure or the early stage of rosacea. Congestion around the upper lip can occur if you used comedogenic lipstick or liners


B E T W E E N - E Y E B R O W S
The middle of the forehead, between eyebrows is related to the liver. This zone can indicate over consumption of alcohol, rich foods and possibly food allergies such as lactose intolerant.
