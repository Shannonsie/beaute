Hello, I am Shannon!

I live in Jakarta and currently studying in Saint Ursula Senior High School. These days, I feel that we, women often feel insecure because of the beauty standard that's built in our world. That's why I created Béaute. Béaute is a French word that means beauty. As its name, I created Béaute to help women realize their "beauty" inner and outer beauty to become more confident and more self-loving.
