Skincare Steps

.

  1. Cleanser
    • For the first step in applying skincare, you need to clean your face. Because clean skin will help the ingredients to absorb properly.
    • Recommendation : Use cleanser with either alpha hydroxy acids (lactic or glycolic) or beta hydroxy acids (salicyclic) or even better, a combination of the two.


  2. Toner
    • Toners are designed to replenish skin through hydration and remove dead cells and dirt left behind after cleansing.
    • Recommendation : Use toner straight after cleansing and skip if you have dry skin.


  3. Serums
    • Serum contain ingredients that will protect skin against damage caused by unstable molecules known as free radicals.
    • Pat a few drops onto your face and neck.


  4. Eye Cream
    • An eye cream can brigthen, smooth and firm up the area around your eyes.
    • Recommendation : Use your ring finger to dab a small amount of eyecream onto the eye area.


  5. Spot Treatments
    • Spot treatments work by delivering acne fighting ingredients directly to the offending blemish.
    • Spot treatments are most helpful if you only succumb to the occasional zit here and there.


  6. Moisturizer
    • A moisturizer will soothe and soften skin
    • Recommendation : Apply to cheek first, then to the rest of the face using upward strokes.


  7. Face Oil
    • Face Oils are specially tailored to work with your natural oils to help keep your skin balanced.
    • Face Oils especially useful if your skin‚Äôs showing signs of dryness, flakiness, or dehydration


  8. Sunscreen
    • Sunscreen is a product that absorbs or reflects some of the sun's ultraviolet (UV) radiation and thus helps protect against sunburn and also reduce risk of skin cancer
    • Recommendation : Choose sunscreen that rated SPF 30 or higher


  9. Retinol
    • Retinol boosts the amount of collagen your body makes and plumps out skin, cutting down fire lines and wrinkles
    • It also improves skin tone and color and reduces mottled patches.
